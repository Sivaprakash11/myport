MOVIE PASS

MoviePass is an innovative online ticket booking application developed using React.js. Designed to enhance the movie-going experience, MoviePass allows users to effortlessly browse current movie listings, view showtimes, and secure tickets in just a few clicks. The intuitive interface ensures a seamless booking process, from selecting seats to receiving digital tickets. Built with a focus on performance and user experience, MoviePass leverages the power of React.js to provide a dynamic, responsive, and engaging platform for movie enthusiasts.

MUSIC ON

MUSIC ON is a sleek and user-friendly music listening platform built using HTML and CSS. Designed to provide a seamless and enjoyable audio experience, play, and enjoy their favorite tracks. The intuitive layout and stylish design ensure that users can easily navigate through playlists, control playback, and immerse themselves in high-quality music. With MUSIC ON, the joy of listening to music is enhanced by a visually appealing and responsive web design, making it perfect for music enthusiasts seeking both functionality and aesthetics.
